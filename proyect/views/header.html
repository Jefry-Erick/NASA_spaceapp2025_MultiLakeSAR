Quick Guide

  1. Upload CSV/JSON metadata and VV/VH GeoTIFFs, or provide COG URLs.
  2. Click "Validate Dataset" to check files and prepare the map.
  3. Use layer toggles (VV/VH/RGB, Coastline, Reeds) to explore.
  4. Adjust visualization range or presets (Auto, dB, p98) if needed.
  5. Review metrics and charts in the Dashboard for insights.
